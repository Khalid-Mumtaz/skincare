TOTKA NO 1

Sugar – 1 Tablespoon(removes dead skin)
Coconut oil – 1 Teaspoon (nourishes the skin).

Turmeric – 1/4 teaspoon (used for even complexion)
Gram flour –  1 tablespoon (helps reducing facial hair)
Lemon juice – 6-8 drops (main bleaching agent)
Rose water – As per requirement
Before applying or using this face pack, 
wash your facial skin with a face wash. 
Now pour sugar in palm full of coconut oil and 

gently scrub your face with it.
After 2-3 minutes of scrubbing wash your face with water.
Now take a bowl, and mix Gram flour, 
turmeric and lemon juice in it. 
Add a  few drops of Rose water 
and keep mixing till you get a thick paste.
Apply this paste of your face an leave it till dries out naturally. 
After that rinse it with lukewarm water.
